About Me

I began dancing at six, but only started training seriously at eleven. that may be considered late in the dance world, but it didnt stop me This is a photo of me and my dad at one of my first recitals—when I knew I’d found what I loved.

I trained at Dance Theatre of Harlem and moved quickly through the levels. After COVID, I was accepted into LaGuardia High School and Ballet Academy East. I’ve attended summer intensives at Alonzo King LINES Ballet and Atlanta Ballet, each helping me grow as an artist and a person.
